Thomas' Favorite Movies as of 11/6/2023

My Top 3 Movies

HackSaw Ridge

The film focuses on the World War II experiences of Desmond Doss, an American pacifist combat medic who, as a Seventh-day Adventist Christian, refused to carry or use a weapon or firearm of any kind.

Princess Mononoke

We bought this film for my wife and we both really enjoyed it!

A Silent Voice

It follows the story of a former bully turned social outcast, who decides to reconnect and befriend the deaf girl he had bullied years prior.
